Triggers EEG Many Steps Oddball Paradigm

- sta: standard tone
- tar: target tone
- 1 instruction: first instruction
- restInstruct: instruction for eye artefact & resting measurement
- blinksInstruct: instruction for blinks
- blinksStart: instruction to start blinking
- blinksEnd: instruction to stop blinking
- EyesMoveInstruct: instruction to move eyes from left to right
- EyesMoveStart: instruction to start moving eyes from left to right
- EyesMoveEnd: instruction to stop moving eyes from left to right
- EyesOpenStart: instruction to look straight ahead with open eyes
- EyesClosedStart: instruction to close eyes & sit still for one minute
- Start soon: reminder on the task and that the task starts now
- 11: left side of screen was tapped
- 22: right side of screen was tapped
- 33: screen was tapped
